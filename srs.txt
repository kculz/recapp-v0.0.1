1. Introduction & Purpose
This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) defines the complete functional, non-functional, security, and operational requirements for the Rehabilitation & Counseling Client Portal — hereafter referred to as the System.

1.1 Purpose
The System is a secure, organisation-exclusive digital platform designed to support individuals undergoing rehabilitation programmes or receiving counseling services. It provides clients with private tools to document their personal recovery journeys, engage with certified counselors, connect with peer communities, and access real-time communication facilities.

1.2 Scope
	•	The System is available only to clients registered by the organisation; no public self-registration is permitted.
	•	It serves two primary client tracks: Rehabilitation Clients and Counseling Clients.
	•	Counselors and administrators operate the System through elevated-access dashboards.
	•	The platform spans mobile (iOS & Android) and web (desktop/tablet) surfaces.
